# ReviewGhost — System Limitations & Safety

## Technical Limitations
1. **JavaScript Heavy Sites:** Sites that require complex user interaction (beyond simple scrolls) may return UNKNOWN.
2. **Deep Captchas:** High-tier security (e.g. DataDome) may temporarily block extraction cycles.
3. **Low Review Volume:** Products with < 10 reviews will always return UNKNOWN due to statistical insignificance.

## Safety & Compliance
1. **Non-Accusatory Tone:** We never use the word "fraud" or "fake" in user-facing verdicts.
2. **Probability-Based:** All outputs are presented as "trust signals" and "patterns," not absolute facts.
3. **Data Freshness:** We cannot guarantee real-time parity with the live site if caching is active.

## What We Do Not Guarantee
- Product safety or physical quality.
- Accuracy of seller-provided metadata (e.g. "Genuine Leather").
- Refund or warranty claim success.
